in order to make the cards truly resonate with your family's unique tapestry of experiences, we'd love to hear a little about your stories, your shared laughter and tears, your interests, and the unique ways in which you communicate.

your shared stories are safe with us, like cherished letters in a sealed box. we promise that they'll be used only to create your personalized card game and nothing else.

if you're ready to share, please continue. remember, this is your journey, and you can pause or stop anytime you want.
